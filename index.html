About Me

Quick Introduction

My name is Bill Zhou and I am currently a student studying Computer Science at UC Berkeley. Outside of, I am working on computer vision research with Dr. Allen Yang and serve as the Student Outreach Coordinator in the ASUC (Associated Students of the University of California). Here is a quick list of facts about me.

  • I love traveling and photography
  • I am a nationally ranked table tennis player
  • I can solve a Rubik's Cube in 31 seconds
  • I am experienced with C++, Python, Java application developement
  • My goal is to build an AI as powerful as J.A.R.V.I.S. from Iron Man
